Why Christianity?

2017 April 14-17 - Drakudden, Stockholm

Register

What is Easter Camp?

Four days of exploring life's big questions, making friends along the way.

Why should I come?

At some point in your life, you might've thought about what the meaning of life is. Our parents might tell us it's about studying, finding a good job, and having a family. Our friends might tell us it's to have fun, and experience the world. But inside of you, there is a nagging feeling that maybe there's something more.

This Easter you can join us for a chance to explore the big questions in life, and why you should consider Christianity.

I'm not a Christian, can I still join?

Yes, and bring your friends!

How do I get to camp?

Arrive in Stockholm by 15:00 on Friday, March 14th

Departure from Stockholm after 14:00 on Monday, March 17th

Book bus, train or flights to Stockholm, get to Stockholm Central Station, and we'll arrange for someone to bring you to the Camp Site, called Drakudden, which is about an hour outside of Stockholm.

How much does it cost?

About 700 SEK

500 SEK camp fee + 200 SEK transport from Stockholm City to the camp site. In addition you'll have to arrange transportation from your home to Stockholm Central Station.

What should I bring?

  • Notebook, pencil/pen
  • Around 200 SEK for the round trip from Stockholm City to the camp site.
  • Pillow case, bed sheet and quilt cover
  • Toiletry (toothbrush, toothpaste, towels, soap/shampoo, etc.)
  • Clothes for different weather
  • Slippers (the floors are cold)
  • Get the Bible App for your phone. If you have a physical Bible, please bring it as well!
